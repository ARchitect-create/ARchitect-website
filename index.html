Frequently Asked Questions

What are "Video Translation Hours"?

The amount of video content you can convert into AR guides each month. For example, 5 hours/month means you can process up to 5 hours of engineering videos into interactive AR experiences.

What are "AR Guide Usage Hours"?

The amount of time your team can actively use AR guides in the field. One hour of AR usage equals one hour of real-world guidance provided to your users with our platform.

How do Credit Rollovers work?

Unused hours from your monthly allocation roll over to the next month, so you never lose your investment. Available in Advanced and Premium plans.

What's included in Backend Support?

Priority technical assistance for model training, integration issues, and optimization. Our engineers work directly with your team to ensure success.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. You can change your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle with prorated adjustments.

Is there a contract or long-term commitment?

No contracts required. All plans are month-to-month with the flexibility to cancel anytime. No hidden fees or penalties.
