How ARchitect Works

A seamless process from video to AR guidance in minutes

1

Upload Your Video

Share your engineering process video in any format and duration. Our system processes your content securely.

2

AI Concept Breakdown

Advanced LLM analyzes your video and breaks down the engineering process into clear, actionable steps automatically.

3

Video Segmentation

System automatically divides your video by identified steps, creating training data for machine learning models.

4

Model Training

Multiple machine learning models train on segmented video data for optimal accuracy and performance.

5

AR Export

Your guidance is converted into a lightweight, optimized AR format file ready for deployment.

6

AR Glasses Ready

Upload to your AR glasses and receive step-by-step guidance in real-time during field operations.

7

Gesture Navigation

Navigate steps with natural pinky swipes, detected by our advanced gesture recognition model.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are "Video Translation Hours"?

The amount of video content you can convert into AR guides each month. For example, 5 hours per month means you can process up to 5 hours of engineering videos into interactive AR experiences.

What are "AR Guide Usage Hours"?

The amount of time your team can actively use AR guides in the field. One hour of usage equals one hour of real-world guidance provided to users with our platform.

How do Credit Rollovers work?

Unused hours from your monthly allocation roll over to the next month, so you never lose your investment. This is available in the Advanced and Premium plans.

What's included in Backend Support?

Priority technical assistance for model training, integration issues, and optimization. Our engineers work directly with your team to ensure successful implementation.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. You can change your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle with prorated adjustments as needed.

Is there a contract or commitment required?

No contracts are required. All plans are month-to-month with complete flexibility to cancel anytime. No hidden fees or penalties.
